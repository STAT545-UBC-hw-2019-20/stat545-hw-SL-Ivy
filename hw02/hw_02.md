#### 1.3 Filter `gapminder` to all entries that have experienced a drop in life expectancy.


```r
gapminder %>%
  arrange(year) %>% 
  group_by(country) %>% 
  mutate(diff_LifeExp = lifeExp - lag(lifeExp)) %>% 
  filter(diff_LifeExp < 0) %>% 
  arrange(country) %>%
  knitr::kable(format = 'html', align = 'c')
```
